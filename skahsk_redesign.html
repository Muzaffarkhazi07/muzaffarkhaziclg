Who We Are

A Legacy of Excellence
in Education

Shri Kadasiddheshwar Arts College and H.S. Kotambri Science Institute stands as one of Karnataka's most distinguished institutions of higher learning. Under the stewardship of the K.L.E. Society, we have nurtured generations of scholars, scientists, and leaders.

Our commitment to academic rigour, holistic development, and community engagement has earned us the prestigious NAAC A+ accreditation — a testament to the relentless dedication of our faculty and the brilliance of our students.

Offering programs in Arts and Sciences, we create thinkers who challenge boundaries and graduates who transform their communities.

🎯 Our Vision

"To be a centre of excellence, committed to nurturing value-based education, research, and social responsibility."

🚀 Our Mission

"To provide quality education, foster critical thinking, and build competent citizens who contribute meaningfully to society."
